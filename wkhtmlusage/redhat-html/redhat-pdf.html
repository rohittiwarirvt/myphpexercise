Short Description

Partner Link - The official mobile app for connecting Red Hat Partners

Project Objectives

We have recognized the need for a partner mobile application for two reasons:

  • We are living in the era of mobility and business happens on the move. We need to empower our partners to do business with Red Hat real time and without the constraints of the desktop.
  • Weâ€™ve received loud and clear partner feedback that they do not have enough relevant communication touch points with Red Hat and a mobile app is one of the most strategic tools for deep engagement and targeted information delivery.

GOALS:

  • Increase adoption and usage of select RH Connect business tools by 20%
  • Increase partner satisfaction on the communication scale in our annual sat survey
  • Increase number of users per account on RH Connect for Business Partners by 10%
